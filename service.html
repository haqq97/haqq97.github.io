A Friend In Need Is A Friend Indeed

I am blessed to have parents who taught me the importance of helping others and giving back to the community at a very young age. I have found that true fulfillment lies in the service of others and I aim to continue my involvement in community service and volunteering work.
GULF COAST REGIONAL BLOOD CENTER
A blood donation to someone is a gift of life and one single blood donation can save up to 3 lives! I donate blood every 6 months and starting from my next birthday I aim to donate blood every 3 months.
BIG EVENT
Every year I take part in the largest student-run project in the nation which has allowed me to work on service projects involving yard-work, gardening and landscaping. I enjoy meeting our residents and putting a smile on their faces.
ADOPT-A-STREET - AGGIE ROTERACT CLUB
I was part of the Aggie Roteract Club for 2 years and I had the opportunity to participate in the Adopt-A-Street program during which we helped keep our streets clean and beautiful.
SPIRIT OF TEXAS FESTIVAL - AGGIE ROTERACT CLUB
As volunteers for the event, we had a unique opportunity to help organizers create the largest chili in the world! It was a thrilling experience.
DUKE OF EDINBURGH GOLD AWARD
As part of the Residential Project of the DoE Program, I had the opportunity to lead a group of 8 students in a remote village to document the challenges faced by a non-profit disability center and its patients to create awareness and find potential solutions.
