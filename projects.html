AT&T
ALERT METRICS

Customized an AIOps system to provide metrics that measure the performance of IT operations. Data coming from different AT&T applications was pipelined using Kafka and an application was created that calculates metrics such as Time to Resolution. This application will auto-assign tasks for developers and optimize decision-making by allocating resources based on priority.

AT&T
CLASSROOM ENGAGEMENT TOOL

The AT&T Intern Innovation Challenge took place between interns all over the US and our team developed a solution that assists educators in conducting virtual classrooms. By using sentiment-analysis, eye-detection tools and speech recognition mechanisms, our software can generate engagement metrics for classrooms which will enable teachers to measure their classroom's engagement. We envisioned that our tool will integrate with any video-conferencing software and provide real-time engagment analysis with tips that teachers can utilize to make their classrooms more engaging!

ENGR-112
DSTR ROBOT

I had a unique opportunity during my freshman year to work on a "Systems of Systems" Project brought together by Texas Instruments and the Department of Engineering. Our team engineered a four-wheeled racing robot by implementing the Mechanical, Electrical, and Controls Sub-assemblies. I learned how to use power tools such as soldering iron and drills to put together the mechanical assembly which was a new experience for me. We used MATLAB to implement the RC Unit that controls the robot wirelessly and Energia to collect data from the Embedded TI mirocontroller on the robot
